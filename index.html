About Us

At charity: water, we believe that everyone deserves clean water! Every person on Earth should have access to safe, drinkable water — and we’re on a mission to make that happen.

100% of public donations have gone directly to funding water projects that transform lives. From rural villages to remote communities, we partner with local experts to bring clean water where it’s needed most — creating lasting change that ripples through health, education, and opportunity.

Join us. Change lives — one drop at a time.
